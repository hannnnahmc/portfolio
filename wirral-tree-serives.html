Website build from scratch for a local tree surgeon trying to boost his online presence to increase business.

Background

Wirral Tree Services is a local tree surgery business who relied mostly on word-of-mouth and brochure marketing. To continue it's growth, the owner wanted to be online and ranking well in search engine results.

Project Goals

  • Build an easy-to-use website for all ages to be able to achieve a certain goal
  • Create user personas to structure marketing decisions
  • Use SEO to rank well in search engine results
